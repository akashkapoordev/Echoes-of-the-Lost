A beginner-to-advanced Unity game development course where you build **one unique portfolio game** from scratch over 8 weeks.

**The Game:** You are a cartographer in a world of void. As you walk, terrain materializes under your feet. Your movement pattern determines what biome appears. Creatures lurk dormant in the void. You name the landmarks you discover. Ghost echoes of other explorers drift through your world.

**Your Starting Level:** C# basics (variables, loops, classes) + Unity tutorials followed along

**Target Level:** Ship a complete, unique game to [itch.io](http://itch.io) with a portfolio page

**Schedule:** 8 weeks, 3–4 hours/day
